Welcome to Our Salon

Candy’s Dog Salon is a long-standing neighborhood grooming shop known for its hands-on care, friendly atmosphere, and dependable service for dogs of all sizes. Operating as a traditional local small business, the salon has built a loyal customer base through personalized attention and consistent grooming quality rather than a corporate-style pet spa experience.

The Candy Touch

Reviews consistently highlight Candy's gentle handling, patience, and deep experience with dogs of different temperaments and breeds. Nervous or difficult dogs response wonderfully to her calm approach.

Warm & Clean

Customers frequently describe our shop environment as warm, welcoming, and pristine. Our friendly staff works hard to provide a genuine sense of care for every single animal that walks through our door.

Happy & Relaxed

We've earned a standout reputation for compassionate treatment. Many pet owners note that their dogs leave looking adorable, polished, and—most importantly—visibly comfortable and relaxed after appointments.

Our Services

We provide comprehensive, breed-specific grooming customized completely to your dog’s coat type, size, and temperament needs.

Full Grooming

Tailored coat trimming, styling, and breed-specific grooming cuts designed to keep your dog comfortable and looking their absolute best.

Bathing & Washing

Thorough full-service deep cleaning wash using gentle, animal-safe formulations that match your dog's skin sensitivity.

Essential Nail & Coat Care

Professional nail care, detailed coat de-shedding, and ear checks to keep your pup's foundational health tracked and clean.
